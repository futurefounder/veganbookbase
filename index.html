💡 About

What is the goal of site?

"Record 500,000 people pledge to eat only vegan food in January"
- The Guardian 2021

Situation

Choosing a plant-based has already benefited millions of people. More and more people are joining the vegan-lifestyle every year.
On the vegan journey knowledge and education is a major factor in understanding the effect food & diet has on yourself, other beings and the planet.

Problem

But, searching for vegan books online can become really overwhelming.
There are huge resources like the VeganCheatSheet.org., but it is hard to get a quick overview.

Solution

That is why this website is trying to present a selection of curated vegan books in a easy to understand, skimmable manner.
The categories are supposed to give you a first overview of how to enter the vegan door.
These categories are by no means absolute, each title has a little of every topic.
But, you might find a better access by choosing the topic you are interested into the most.
Note on the information:
I have tried to always link to the websites of the authors, as they have further information on their book and links on their preferred online store.
If I did not find an authors' website there is a link to good reads.
