💡 About

What is this site?

"Record 500,000 people pledge to eat only vegan food in January" - The Guardian 2021 As more and more people are discovering a vegan lifestyle the wish to dive deeper into some topics by reading books has emerged. Searching for vegan books online can become really overwhelming. That is why this website is trying to present a selection of curated vegan book in a easy to understand, skimmable manner. By this everyone thinking about vegan topics has the chance to explore and discover different books. There are a lot of vegan books to discover. If you want to have a look into a big list check out the VeganCheatSheet.org.
